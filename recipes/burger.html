Burger Recipe

Description

The three best foods in the world combined in a juicy grilled cheeseburger! These go great with Cowboy Mashed Potatoes!

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh jalapeno chile pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 4 slices pepper jack cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to 425
  2. Brush grill grates with oil
  3. Mix jalapeno pepper, garlic, onion, salt, pepper and ground beef in a medium bowl.
  4. Form into four patties
  5. Grill hamburger patties 5 mins per side, or until well done
  6. Top with pepper jack cheese, and serve on toasted brioche buns
