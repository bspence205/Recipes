Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Description

Extra creamy buttermilk mashed potatoes bursting with flavor. No need to add gravy!

Ingredients

  • 6 medium white potatoes, peeled
  • Â¾ cup buttermilk
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons chicken bouillon powder
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 20 to 30 minutes.
  3. Drain and mash in a large bowl.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
